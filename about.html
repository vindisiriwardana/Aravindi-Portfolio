About Me

I am an enthusiastic Information Technology undergraduate currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Information Technology at SLIIT. I am passionate about building user-friendly web applications and solving real-world problems through technology.

My technical expertise includes Full-Stack Web Development using MERN stack, Java development, MySQL database management, and Software Testing methodologies including Manual Testing and Agile Testing. I have hands-on experience working with modern development tools such as Visual Studio Code, Eclipse, GitHub, and Apache Tomcat.


Throughout my academic journey, I have contributed to several projects including a Travel and Tourism Management System, Online Gaming Web Application, and a Hotel Reservation System. In these projects, I actively supported full CRUD operations, user profile management, cart systems, and rental handling features.

I also hold certifications in Agile Testing, ISTQB Foundation preparation, Apache POI Masterclass, Manual Testing, and Data Engineering Foundations, which have strengthened my understanding of software quality assurance, structured development processes, and data management concepts.

I am a creative and analytical thinker who enjoys continuous learning and adapting to new technologies. My goal is to grow as a Full-Stack Developer or Quality Engineering professional while contributing to innovative, scalable, and efficient software solutions.
